Sacred Grocery

A Cambridge, MA grocery store based on the principles of Sacred Economics

Charles Eisenstein's Sacred Economics envisions a world where we peg value to the beauty of our natural surroundings and gift economies thrive alongside traditional monetary systems. This thought experiment and visualization, a final project for the MAS.S70 Liberating Structures seminar at the MIT Media Lab, is an exploration of how this vision could be implemented in a hypothetical grocery store in Cambridge, MA.
