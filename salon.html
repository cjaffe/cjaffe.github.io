Salon #13 - 5/6/15

Salon #14 - 6/3/15

Though a slightly haphazard setting with the couch and red chair gone, we had a lovely happy group. Zoe read a fantastic Hemingway fairytale/ parable about a fighting bull from a feminist storybook called "The Outspoken Princess and The Gentle Knight" (which I will definitely be reading to my future children). Then, Caroline J. read an essay called "Mission and Metrics" from her advisor, Sep Kamvar's, essay book. We discussed how bad metrics can sometimes be antithetical to the purpose and goals of different organizations, cited examples, and pondered how to fix the metrics problem. Next time we (may) break from the regular salon schedule for a summer picnic, and to tearfully say goodbye to all the people departing for new cities, and then we'll be back the following time with our regular programming.


Salon Outing - 6/17/15

Six of us ventured out of Cambridge to attend one of the Boston Public Library's "Concerts in the Courtyard". The band was ChoroBop, a fantastic group of 3 Brazilian Berklee students who played a mix of choro and jazz. The courtyard was a beautiful, serene setting. Afterwards, we ate a lively dinner at nearby Casa Romero.
